# Week 3 Workshop

## Scenario

You work as an analyst for the New Zealand (NZ) Transport Agency Waka Kotahi (NZTA). In 2024, the NZ Government released the New Zealand’s Road Safety Objectives that outlines a plan over the next three years for tackling road safety issues. The following objectives are included in the document:

- **Safer roads: lift the quality of our road infrastructure** involves retrofitting safety interventions in high-crash areas.
- **Safer drivers: ensure road users are alert, unimpaired and comply with the road rules** focuses on additional investment in road policing and enforcement.

To implement actions in support of these objectives, you have been tasked with understanding trends in crashes and driving offences. Your manager is also interested in understanding whether there are specific locations that should be prioritised for intervention and whether the type of interventions to introduce may need to differ across regions. You have been asked to create a Power BI report and present on your findings.

## Data

The data used in this session is based on a subset of the Crash Analysis System (CAS) data from the NZTA and road policing driver offence data from the NZ Police. It comprises information on traffic crashes and driving offences across the Auckland, Bay of Plenty, Canterbury, Otago, Waikato, and Wellington regions from 2017 to 2026 (Q1).

Download the files from the `data/` folder:

- `week3_DimRegion.csv`
- `week3_FactCrash.csv`
- `week3_FactOffence.csv`
